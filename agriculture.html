🌱About Space Agriculture🌱

Space agriculture is the practice of growing food and cultivating plants in space environments, such as aboard spacecraft, space stations, or future habitats on the Moon and Mars. It plays a crucial role in supporting long-term space missions by providing fresh food, recycling air and water, and reducing dependence on Earth-based supplies. This field combines advanced technologies in hydroponics, aeroponics, and controlled environments to overcome challenges like microgravity and limited resources. Space Agriculture.
